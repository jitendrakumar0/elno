Modular Wardrobe

Being the first brand to introduce the concept of modular kitchens in India, Elnokitchens has consistently redefined its product range to offer the best to the Indian consumer. Now, Elnokitchens brings to you its latest innovation, with a premium range of wardrobes. Every Elnokitchens wardrobe features exquisiteness that is set to define your style persona in its every bit. You do not just bring Elnokitchens wardrobe, you bring home the years of expertise of craftsmanship that guarantees custom-made living solutions at the highest standards of functionality, workmanship and design.

Fitted wardrobes mean storage that really matches your space. Ours also mean you can choose frames and doors that match your style and interior fittings that suit what you wear. Start with our pre-designed combinations. You can adapt them to suit yourself or go all the way and create your own tailor-made wardrobe with our designers. Our specialist designers will take time to talk to you about your fitted furniture needs while you can select from a wide selection of wardrobes' layouts.
